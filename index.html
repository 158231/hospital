Hospitals




As part of its integrated academic health system, Dubai Health provides inpatient, outpatient and emergency department services across 6 hospitals within the Emirate of Dubai. These facilities deliver care across a diverse set of specialties. Dubai Health's hospitals are all Joint Commission International (JCI) accredited and are awarded the ranking of stage 6 within the Electronic Medical Record Adoption Model (EMRAM) from the Healthcare Information and Management Systems Society (HIMSS).
